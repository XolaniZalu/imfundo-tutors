Position Overview

We are currently seeking a talented and enthusiastic tutor to join our team. In this role, you will have the opportunity to work one-on-one with students, helping them achieve their academic goals and unlock their full potential. Whether it's math, science, language arts, or test preparation, you will have the chance to make a meaningful impact on tutees' lives through personalized instruction and support.


We are current recruiting tutors for the following modules:

  • Financial Accounting
  • Cost and Management Accounting
  • Auditing and Governance
  • Taxation

However, should you want to collaborate with us for matters involving but not limited to:

  • Advertising your tutorial related services;
  • Hosting your tutorial services with us;
  • And more services that relates to our business objective.

Then you are more than welcome to email us at Imfundotutors@gmail.com

Please note that tutors will be compansated based on hours worked and not on a fixed monthly rate basis.

Responsibilities

  • Provide individualized tutoring sessions tailored to each tutee's learning needs and goals.
  • Develop and implement engaging lesson plans and teaching strategies to enhance comprehension and retention.
  • Assess tutees' progress and adjust teaching methods accordingly to ensure academic success.
  • Communicate effectively with tutees.

Requirements

  • At least 75% must have been achieved in the desired module.
  • Previous tutoring or teaching experience preferred.
  • Strong subject knowledge and expertise in one or more academic areas.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Passion for education and a commitment to helping students succeed.

Benefits

  • Flexible schedule with the opportunity to set your own hours.
  • Professional development opportunities to expand your skills and knowledge.
  • The rewarding experience of making a positive impact on students' lives.
